An Introduction

I started coding in October of 2019 in an effort to transition careers from being a Polysomnographic Technologist working in a Sleep Lab into something more fulfilling and interesting. I decided I would pursue web development for better or worse, but found coding to be a sweet spot between beautiful, infuriating, challenging and rewarding. I enrolled in Nucamp's fullstack bootcamp, not knowing what "HTML" meant or what a "function" was, and graduated from that program in April 2020
